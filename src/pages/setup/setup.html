Mem App

A memorization app to help with classical buddhist texts. Hopefully there is something special about this endevor.

This app features a simple interface.

Instructions

Tapping (clicking) on a line will progessively hide a selection of characters on the line with an underscore. For example, the words "hidden text" might be transformed into "h_____ t___" where only the first letter of each word is show and all other letters are hidden.

Tap-and-hold (click-and-hold) will do the opposite of just a tap.

On start, the app will resume to the last place you were at. If you wish to restart, come back to this setup page and hit Restart.
